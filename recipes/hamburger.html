Homemade Hamburger

Description

Here is the very best homemade burger recipes. With these chewing-ready burgers, you will fill up your tummy in no time with great satisfaction.

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg, beaten
  • 1 tablespoon ketchup
  • ¼ cup plain bread crumbs
  • ⅔ cup chopped cooked bacon
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar

Steps

  1. Whisk egg and brown sugar in a bowl
  2. Add breadcrumbs and chopped cooked bacon
  3. Form mixture into 4 burgers with your wet hands
  4. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate
  5. Place burgers on the grate and cook, turing occasionally, until firm and cooked to your desired doneness
  6. Let burgers rest for a few minutes before serving
